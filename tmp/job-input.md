## Job Description

> Paste the **full job description** below, including responsibilities, required skills, desired skills, and education requirements. The agent uses this to extract keywords, map experience to requirements, and align the resume content.

```
Our client is seeking a Lead Cloud Architect to support a large federal cloud operations and migration program. This role focuses on enterprise-scale Azure infrastructure, post-migration optimization, governance, and cost control within a mature multi-cloud environment.

This is not an application development role. It is focused on infrastructure engineering, cloud operations, and optimization at scale.

Key Responsibilities

• Provide architecture and engineering leadership across a large Azure enterprise environment
• Support post-migration optimization across lift-and-shift and refactored workloads
• Drive cloud cost optimization strategy including Reserved Instances, Savings Plans, and subscription governance
• Design and govern enterprise landing zone architecture aligned to Azure best practices
• Lead network and IP strategy including CIDR planning, hub-and-spoke governance, and IP scarcity mitigation
• Oversee Azure Management Group hierarchy and subscription segmentation
• Enforce RBAC, Azure Policy, tagging governance, and least-privilege models
• Support hybrid cloud architecture across Azure and legacy data centers
• Guide monitoring and observability strategy using Azure Monitor and enterprise tools
• Partner with security, network, PMO, and compliance teams in a regulated environment

Required Experience

• 10+ years of IT experience with strong Azure infrastructure depth
• Experience operating in medium to large enterprise Azure environments
• Deep understanding of cloud optimization and FinOps at production scale
• Experience with Reserved Instance and Savings Plan modeling
• Strong Azure networking experience including VNET design, peering, private endpoints, and NAT planning
• Experience with infrastructure-as-code tools such as Terraform, ARM, or Ansible
• Familiarity with Azure Monitor and enterprise monitoring platforms
• US Citizen with ability to obtain Public Trust clearance

Nice to Have

• Experience in federal or regulated environments
• Multi-cloud exposure including AWS
• Experience with Azure DevOps pipelines or GitOps practices

Fully remote. Stable long-term program. Strategic visibility.
```
